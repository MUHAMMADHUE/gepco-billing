How to Check Your GEPCO Bill Online?

Follow these simple steps to check and download your GEPCO bill:

  1. Visit the Official Website – Go to the GEPCO Bill Check page.
  2. Enter Your Reference Number – Find your 14-digit reference number on your previous bill and enter it in the provided field.
  3. Click on 'Check Bill' – After entering the number, click on the check button.
  4. View & Download Your Bill – Your bill will appear on the screen. You can download it in PDF format or print it for future reference.

Benefits of Checking GEPCO Bill Online

  • ✔ No need to visit the office.
  • ✔ Instant access to your bill anytime.
  • ✔ Save your bill in digital format for record-keeping.

FAQs

1. Where can I find my reference number?
Your reference number is available on the top-left corner of your previous GEPCO bill.

2. Can I check my GEPCO bill without a reference number?
No, a reference number is mandatory to retrieve your bill.

3. Is it safe to check my bill online?
Yes, as long as you use the official GEPCO website or a trusted source.
